On September 17, 2014, the Board of Directors (Board) adopted the CSR Policy (Policy), which is published on the Company's website. The Tata Group's key programmes - Education, Health, Livelihood, Rural and Urban Infrastructure – are aligned with the Company's CSR. It also makes initiatives in sports, disaster assistance, the environment, and ethnicity, all with the goal of enhancing people's lives.
