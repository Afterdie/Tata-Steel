Ethical behaviour is intrinsic to our business and has been a part of our inheritance since beginning, as visualized by our Founder, Jamsetji Tata. He believed that a business should operate in an exceedingly manner specified it respects the rights of all its stakeholders and creates overall price for society.

Each year, we tend to observe the month of Gregorian calendar month as ‘Ethics Month’ across the organisation to strengthen our moral culture. This year’s theme ‘Integrity Matters’ intends to any drive a way of private commitment, amongst all stakeholders, towards a stronger and moral tomorrow.

The values and principles that have governed our business for a century are enshrined in the Tata Code of Conduct (TCoC).

We abide by the values to ensure a safe workplace and the happiness of employees.
