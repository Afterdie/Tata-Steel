The commitment to zero harm to the individuals we work with and society at large drives Tata Steel's safety and health responsibilities. We aim to attain this goal with the help of a solid management system architecture and a solid safety governance structure. Six long-term safety strategy priorities have been established at the deployment level. Improve competitiveness by developing (safety) leadership capability at all levels.

The Board's Safety, Health, and Environment Committee (headed by an Independent Director) and the Apex Safety Council provide a strong governance structure (Chaired by the Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director). Their orders are passed down through sub-committees chaired by relevant Vice Presidents, who subsequently carry them out across the organisation in monthly reviews.
